About Zach Yu

    Hello, and thank you for taking the time to view my online portfolio. My name is PoYing Yu. You also can call me Zach. I am a student at British Colombia Institute of Technology. Luckily, while I am learning Digital Circuit Design, I began to touch some coding. I discovered that I wanted to be a programmer for my future career.

I could see that in the coding world, we would't only compete or coordinate with people who have same background. The broad Internet is taking everyone together. Programmer should learn how to work with any national and cultural people. Therefore, Vancouver was my perfect choice.

Another reason for this online portfolio is that I am seeking to advance my professional career. I have many skills which make me a very valuable asset to an employer.
